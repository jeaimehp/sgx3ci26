## Overview

SGX3 workforce development programs introduce students and faculty to science gateways, HPC concepts, reproducible research, and practical software development.

My work focuses on helping participants move from exposure to production-oriented skills through structured training, hackathons, project milestones, and research-aligned deliverables.

## Contributions

- Design training agendas and project workflows.
- Support hackathon planning and student team structures.
- Develop curriculum materials for HPC, AI, and science gateway topics.
- Align student work with conference-ready outputs.
