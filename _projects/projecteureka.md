## Overview

projectEUREKA! represents a practical approach to making HPC and AI resources more accessible for research, education, and campus computing environments.

My work in this area includes systems analysis, technical storytelling, platform positioning, and support materials that explain how research computing tools can move from pilot deployments to usable services.

## Contributions

- Translate technical infrastructure into clear user-facing language.
- Support conference and outreach materials.
- Document deployment goals, training needs, and adoption pathways.
- Connect platform capabilities to workforce development outcomes.
